FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers about buying & selling kids' items

How do I sell my kids' outgrown items?

▼

It's easy! Click "Sell Now" in the menu, take a few photos of your item, add a description and price, and publish. Your listing goes live instantly for other parents to see.

Are all items checked for safety?

▼

Yes! We cross-reference all listed items with CPSC recalls. Any item flagged for safety concerns is immediately removed from the marketplace.

What if an item isn't as described?

▼

We offer a 7-day return guarantee. If an item significantly differs from its description, contact us and we'll arrange a full refund or exchange.

Can I sell items in bulk or as a bundle?

▼

Absolutely! Bundles are very popular — grouping by size, season, or theme helps items sell faster. Just create one listing and note it's a bundle in the description.
