Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to our most common questions

How do I create a listing?

▼

Go to the Sell page, fill in your item details, upload photos, set a price, and click Publish. Your listing goes live immediately.

How long does it take to receive payment?

▼

Payouts are processed within 24 hours of a confirmed sale. Funds arrive in your linked account within 1–3 business days.

Is my payment information secure?

▼

Yes. All transactions are encrypted and processed via industry-standard payment gateways. We never store card details on our servers.

What's your return policy?

▼

Buyers can raise a return within 7 days of delivery if the item significantly differs from its description. Our team mediates all disputes fairly.
