Our Story

Keeping Kids' Stuff in the Loop 🌈

Born from the idea that kids outgrow things fast — but great stuff deserves a second life. We're building the most trusted community for parents who love a good deal.

Every Child Deserves Quality 💛

Kids grow fast, and their clothes, toys, and gear shouldn't go to waste. Little Loops makes it easy for families to pass along gently used items to other families who need them — saving money and the planet.

✓Over 45,000 items given a second life
✓Families save an avg. of $1,200/year
✓2,000+ lbs of textile waste prevented monthly
